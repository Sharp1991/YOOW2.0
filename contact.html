Frequently Asked Questions

How do I donate clothes to YOOW?

We accept donations during store hours. Please bring clean, gently used clothing to our store in Police Bazar. We'll provide you with a discount coupon for your next purchase as a thank you!

Do you offer consignment?

Yes! We offer a 50/50 consignment split. Bring your quality items to our store during business hours for evaluation. Items remain in the store for 60 days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI, and all major credit/debit cards. Online orders can be paid via UPI or card.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our shipping options in the future.
