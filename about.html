Our Story

Where second hands become first choice

About YOOW Thrift

YOOW was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make sustainable fashion accessible and desirable. We believe that secondhand doesn't mean second best - in fact, we think preloved clothing can be your first choice for style, quality, and value.

Our journey began in Shillong, where we saw an opportunity to create a curated thrift experience that prioritizes quality, hygiene, and style. Every item in our collection is carefully inspected, cleaned, and prepared to ensure it meets our high standards.

Our Values

Sustainability

Reducing fashion waste by giving clothes a second life. We're committed to promoting circular fashion and minimizing our environmental footprint.

Affordability

Making quality fashion accessible to everyone. We believe great style shouldn't come with a high price tag or cost the earth.

Quality

Discover innovation and cleaning of everything. Each item undergoes rigorous inspection and professional cleaning before reaching you.

Join the Fashion Revolution

At YOOW, we're redefining fashion by making sustainability stylish and accessible. Every preloved item you choose helps reduce waste and supports a circular economy.
