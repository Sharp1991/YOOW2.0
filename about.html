About YOOW Thrift

YOOW was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make sustainable fashion accessible and desirable. We believe that secondhand doesn't mean second best - in fact, we think preloved clothing can be your first choice for style, quality, and value.

Our journey began in Shillong, where we saw an opportunity to create a curated thrift experience that prioritizes quality, hygiene, and style. Every item in our collection is carefully inspected, cleaned, and prepared to ensure it meets our high standards.
